Park Info

The Park Info tab allows a quick and easy way to see where you left characters or who you have in a particular zone. This information is collected passively as you play characters and instantly searchable.

The data points tracked for each character are as follows:

  • Server Name
  • Character Name
  • Zone - the last zone in which the character was played
  • Account - the username of the account to which the character belongs
  • Bind - the bind location for the character, detected by the act of being bound or by the /charinfo command
  • Sky Corpse Timer - the time since the character's last death while in the Plane of Sky, will end at 7 days (when any corpse will have rotted)

If you play characters that are not your own on a frequent basis, it can become burdensome to have them also included on your Park Info tab. Right clicking on an item will allow you to Ingore or Delete an entry. If it is deleted, it will come back the next time you play that character. If it is ignored, it will not be shown unless the Include Ignored button is checked even through repeated usage.
